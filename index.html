About

For fifteen years I have been building analytics infrastructure and designing workforce intelligence systems inside some of Canada's largest enterprises — not as an outside advisor, but as the practitioner accountable for outcomes.

My work sits at the intersection of workforce strategy, people analytics, data science, and AI enablement. I turn complex organizational challenges into clarity — uncovering the insights that reshape how leaders think about their people, their processes, and their future.

I am focused on building the intelligence infrastructure that helps organizations make workforce decisions with the same rigour they bring to finance and operations — and on publishing original frameworks for workforce transformation in the AI era.

That is what I build. That is what this platform is for.
